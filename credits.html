PARTNERS

Pokemon Brick Bronze Reborn

In Pokemon Brick Bronze Reborn you're a 12-year old kid who has dreamed of becoming a Pokemon trainer, traveling alongside your Pokemon. You'll encounter different Pokemon from different regions. You'll sometimes have Jake, who has been a friend of yours since you were kids, accompanying you in some situations and at the same time, occasionally challenging you to battles.

You will face many tough challenges, meet new faces, learn new things, and witness many unexpected events along the way. Please note that though the game is based off the game Pokemon Brick Bronze which was shut down in April 2018 made in Roblox, Pokemon Brick Bronze Reborn is a fangame made in RPG Maker XP with Pokemon Essentials.
